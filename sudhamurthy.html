Sudha Murthy

Introduction

Sudha Murthy was born in 19 August 1950.She is an Indian educator, author.Sudha Murthy is the chairperson of the Infosys Foundation and a member of public health care initiatives of the Gates Foundation.She is married to the Co-founder of Infosys ,N.R.Nayana Murthy.

Challanges faced by Sudha Murthy

Sudha Murthy faced various societal opposition in life where she had to be up against ire of society for challanging the male dominance in spheres of education and employment.

But she always fought and stood up for what was right.Out of 600 students at her engineering college,she was the only girl.
